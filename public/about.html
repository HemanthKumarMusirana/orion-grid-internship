Our Story

Founded in 2020, Orion Grid began with a simple yet powerful idea: to transform how engineering students prepare for their careers. What started as a small initiative has grown into a trusted internship provider, partnering with 20+ engineering colleges across India. Today, we've trained over 500 students and maintain an 85% placement assistance rate, helping young engineers launch successful careers in technology and engineering.
